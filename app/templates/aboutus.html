About Me

Made By:Andy Liu


About Me: My name is Andy Liu, and I am a 12th grader that goes to Oakland Technical highschool. I chose climate change because I wanted to see its long-term effects. I also wanted to see if there were any solutions to climate change already. I was able to find many different answers, but it would take a long time till climate change would be removed. So, now I want to know how would our future generation handle the climate. Would they not care about it and keep burning CO2, or will they speak up and care.


Purpose: The main goal for this website is to inform the youth by making a game that would teach the users about the effects and solution to climate change.
